3. AI Services and Data Processing

When providing AI services, we may process your business data to deliver the requested automation and intelligence features.

Data Processing Principles:

  • Purpose Limitation: Data processed only for agreed services
  • Data Minimization: We access only necessary information
  • Storage Limitation: Data retained only as long as needed
  • Security First: Industry-standard encryption and security measures

Client Data Ownership:

  • You retain full ownership of your business data
  • We act as a processor, not owner, of your information

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

5. Data Security

Security Measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest

  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for all systems

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Clients are responsible for maintaining their own cybersecurity practices.
